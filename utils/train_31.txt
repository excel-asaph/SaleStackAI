[h2]Frequently Asked Questions
[summary]Doesn’t CPM just depend on buying intent? Isn’t there no hack for it?
[p]Not exactly.
[p]Think of it like an auction.
[p]There are uncontrollable factors (your niche, geography, and buying intent) that create a baseline CPM. That’s the minimum you’re working with.
[p]But then there are controllable factors—your ad creatives (copy, images, videos). These determine how much you actually pay inside the auction.
[p]That’s why two advertisers in the same niche can pay completely different CPMs. One bleeds cash, while the other gets rewarded with cheaper traffic.
[p]The truth is:
[p]Everyone does not pay the same.
[p]And if you know how to design ads Meta loves, you can win the auction at a discount.
[p]Smart advertisers don’t just accept CPMs as “fixed.” They figure out how to pay less.
